About KUSHI KID'S Play School

At KUSHI KID'S Play School, we believe in providing a safe, stimulating, and joyful environment where children can explore, discover, and grow. Our dedicated educators foster a love for learning through play-based activities, creative expression, and social interaction.

We are committed to nurturing each child's unique potential, building confidence, and preparing them for a successful transition to formal schooling. Our curriculum is designed to encourage curiosity, critical thinking, and a strong foundation in early literacy and numeracy.

Come join our family and watch your little one sparkle!

Our Programs

Toddler Playgroup (1.5 - 2.5 years)

Focuses on sensory play, motor skill development, and early social interaction in a nurturing environment.

  • Sensory Exploration
  • Basic Motor Skills
  • Socialization

Nursery (2.5 - 3.5 years)

Introduces foundational concepts through storytelling, art, music, and structured play.

  • Early Literacy & Numeracy
  • Creative Arts
  • Language Development

Junior Kindergarten (3.5 - 4.5 years)

Prepares children for kindergarten with enhanced academic readiness and problem-solving skills.

  • Pre-reading & Writing
  • Basic Math Concepts
  • Science Exploration

Senior Kindergarten (4.5 - 5.5 years)

Advanced curriculum focusing on school readiness, critical thinking, and independent learning.

  • Advanced Literacy
  • Problem Solving
  • Social-Emotional Development
